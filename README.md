# IPL_2026_GT
Did Gujarat Titans Become Too Top-Heavy? A Statistical Analysis of IPL 2026 Batting Contributions
Gujarat Titans' Middle Order Scores More in Losses. Here's What That Actually Means.
Introduction
In T20 cricket, a batting lineup is expected to distribute scoring responsibility across the top order, middle order, and finishers. Teams heavily dependent on a few batters may dominate league stages but become vulnerable when those players fail in knockout matches.
The 2026 IPL season produced a fascinating case study. The Gujarat Titans possessed two of the tournament's highest run scorers and one of the strongest batting units overall. Yet an important question emerges:
Was Gujarat Titans' batting success driven primarily by its top order, and did that dependency reduce the impact of its middle order?
To answer this, we examine batting contributions across all IPL teams before applying statistical hypothesis testing to determine whether the Gujarat Titans were significantly more dependent on their top order than their middle order.

Appendix: Code and Methodology
All analysis was performed using Python with the following libraries:
pandas for data manipulation
numpy for numerical calculations
matplotlib/seaborn for visualizations
scipy for statistical analysis

Data period: IPL 2022 & IPL 2026 (Gujarat Titans Team)
